What to expect

These are not newsletters in the conventional sense — not announcements, not content calendars, not curated link dumps. They are letters: personal, considered, written when there is something worth saying.

Each letter might include

  • An essay or long reflection on something I've been thinking through
  • Notes from a recent event, conversation, or collaboration
  • Observations on culture, language, and creative practice
  • Recommendations — a book, a record, a piece of writing — that deserves more attention
  • Behind-the-scenes context on current projects and partnerships

Sent occasionally — perhaps once or twice a month, never more. Your attention is not taken for granted.
